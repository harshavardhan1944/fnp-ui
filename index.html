Checkout add-on · Video upload · Branded recipient experience

Recording Flow

In-app camera or gallery upload. Max 60s. Preview before confirm. Compressed on-device to ≤15MB.

QR Generation

Token-secured URL auto-generated. QR sent to packing team dashboard. Printed on physical gift card.

Privacy Design

URL not guessable. Token expires 30 days post-delivery. No app needed to view — any browser.

Comfort Care Gifting

Care packages · Recurring delivery · Empathy-first UX

🌸 Pilot First · 3–4 Weeks
Brand Language

"Comfort Care" — not "period gifting" in public UI. Warm, inclusive framing. No clinical copy.

Pilot Strategy

Collection page first. Homepage placement only if CTR >3% + conversion >2% for 2 weeks.

Recurring delivery

Monthly subscription mechanic — high LTV potential and strong retention driver for the app.
